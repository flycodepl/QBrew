Introduction

QBrew is a homebrewer's recipe calculator. With it a brewer can formulate new recipes and calculate gravity, bitterness, color and other attributes.

No payment or registration is required for QBrew, but the author would be extremely delighted to receive spontaneous and voluntarily donated bottles filled with homebrew. Photos of such bottles will do in a pinch. If you wish to contribute to the further inebriation of the author, please send mail to <david@usermode.org> requesting a physical address to send the bottles to.

The homepage for QBrew is http://www.usermode.org/code.html

Code and cash contributions will, of course, grease the wheels of QBrew development.
